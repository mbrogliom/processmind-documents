# AI Process Audit

## Overview

AI Process Audit is a consulting service designed to identify opportunities for automation and artificial intelligence within business processes.

The goal is to help organizations understand how AI can improve operational efficiency.

## Scope

The audit typically focuses on:

- document management processes
- information workflows
- data analysis and reporting
- operational procedures

## Activities

1. Process review
2. Identification of automation opportunities
3. AI use case definition
4. High-level solution architecture
5. Implementation roadmap

## Deliverables

At the end of the audit the client receives:

- process analysis report
- list of AI automation opportunities
- recommended technical solutions
- implementation roadmap

## Typical duration

2 – 3 weeks

## Typical outcomes

Clients often identify opportunities such as:

- AI document assistants
- automated reporting systems
- workflow automation tools
- internal knowledge assistants

## Engagement

The AI Process Audit can be delivered remotely or onsite.
